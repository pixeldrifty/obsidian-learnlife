### Q1
---
(a) Jamal has 3 toffees and 7 fudges. Write down the ratio of toffees to fudges. What fraction of Jamal's sweets are fudges?
The ratio is 3:7 so Jamal has 3 toffees for every 7 fudges. As for the fraction, 3 + 7 = 10 so theres 10 sweets in total, so the fraction would be $\frac{7}{10}$.

(b) In the football team there are 5 girls and 6 boys. Write down the ratio of boys to girls. What fraction of the team are girls?
The ratio of boys to girls is 6:5. The fraction of the team that is girls is $\frac5{11}$.

(c) Write down the ratio of triangles to squares. What fraction of the shapes are squares?
The ratio of triangles to squares is 3:2. The fraction of squares is $\frac25$.
