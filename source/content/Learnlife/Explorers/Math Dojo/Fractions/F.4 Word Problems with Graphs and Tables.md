These problems involve ==**interpreting visual data**, such as pie charts, bar graphs, or tables, to answer fraction-related questions.  
You’ll often need to **find fractional parts of totals**== or compare fractions shown in the data.  
Example: If ¼ of a bar graph represents students who play guitar, and there are 40 students, then ¼ × 40 = 10 students.
