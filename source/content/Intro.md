### The Building Blocks

Every word is made of parts. Those parts are called morphemes — the smallest units of meaning.

There are two kinds:

- [[roots|Roots]] — the core of a word, carrying the main meaning
- [[affixes|Affixes]] — pieces that attach to roots to change or add meaning
